Wiii Lab / Da vu tru

SVG va Canvas khac nhau ro nhat khi ban khong ep chung lam mot viec.

Trong lab nay, SVG khong co gang gia lam bo may vat ly, va Canvas khong co gang dong vai mot so do giai thich day nhan. Cung la chu de “Mo phong Da Vu Tru”, nhung ben trai la mot atlas giai thich de doc, nhan, va suy luan; ben phai la mot truong dong de thay nhieu vat the, nhieu quy dao, nhieu cap nhat moi khung hinh. O lane thu ba, cung scene do duoc dong bang thanh video xuat file de ban thay luc nao nen chuyen tu interactive runtime sang mot media co the phat lai, chia se, va dong goi.

SVG sang ro o cho nao? Text, callout, mui ten, branch, timeline, labels, click tung node, va bo cuc giai thich trong suot.
Canvas sang ro o cho nao? Hang tram hat, motion blur, quy dao song song, truong nang luong rung dong lien tuc theo thoi gian thuc.
Thu nghiem de nhin ra su khac biet Tang Branches, tang Particles, bat Zoom, roi click vao mot vu tru SVG, keo chuot o Canvas, roi export sang video.

SVG Motion Explainer

Moi vu tru la mot node that, co label that, co duong noi that, va co pulse chay doc branch. Ban co the click tung nhanh de doc ghi chu va thay selected state. Day la kieu render rat hop cho visual giai thich co chuyen dong nhe cua Wiii.

SVG
SVG gioi o “motion co nghia”

Moi circle, line, text, badge, callout la mot phan tu DOM/co cau that. Ban de dang them tooltip, highlight, annotation, screen-reader summary, va patch lai tung nhan ma khong pha bo cuc. Chuyen dong nhe van gan voi structure, khong bi troi thanh “hieu ung rong”.

Danh cho Wiii

Neu Wiii dang giai thich “co che”, “so sanh”, “timeline”, “bottleneck”, “architecture”, thi day la lane nen uu tien: gon, sach, article-first, de doc, de sua, va rat Claude-like.

Exported Video Lane

Day khong con la runtime de suy nghi cung nguoi dung nua. Day la luc Wiii dong bang mot khoanh khac cua simulation thanh media phat lai. Video rat hop de chia se, gan vao bai giang, hoac luu lai mot motion explainer da chot.

WebM / MP4 lane
Chua co video export. Bam “Ghi 4 giay WebM” de record truc tiep tu Canvas simulation. Ban se thay ngay khac biet: runtime live co the tuong tac, con video thi duoc dong bang thanh mot media on dinh de phat lai.

Trang thai: Chua export. WebM la demo native trong browser; voi production, Wiii co the transcode sang MP4 o backend neu can compatibility rong hon.

Khi nao nen chuyen thanh video?

Khi ban da chot mot motion va muon phat lai no theo cung mot timeline cho moi nguoi xem, hoac muon gui cho LMS, slide, hoac tai lieu hoc tap ma khong can live runtime.

Danh cho Wiii

Wiii nen giu SVG va Canvas cho giai doan doi thoai va tuong tac. Video nen la lane xuat ban: “dong goi ket qua” sau khi user va agent da chinh xong motion hay simulation.

Decision rule

Neu Wiii muon tot hon, dung dung renderer cho dung bai toan.

Sai lam pho bien la ep Code Studio/HTML freestyle lam tat ca. Cach dung hon la: chart va figure giai thich thi SVG-first, simulation va field dynamics thi Canvas/app-first, artifact va widget tool thi di lane rieng.

Khi nao nen chon SVG

  • Can nhieu nhan, callout, mui ten, chu thich, timeline, architecture, process.
  • Can mot visual inline sach se, net, trong, de “hoa vao bai viet”.
  • Can patch tung claim, tung node, tung label sau follow-up prompt.
  • Can accessibility va screen-reader summary de dang hon.

Khi nao nen chon Canvas

  • Can motion lien tuc, loop vat ly, particle field, trail, va redraw moi frame.
  • Can cam giac “song” hon la “tai lieu hoa”.
  • Can scene nang hon, nhieu object chay dong song song.
  • Can app/simulation interaction nhu keo-tha, perturb, reset, play/pause.

Khi nao nen chon Video

  • Can mot ket qua co the phat lai, gui di, nhung vao slide, LMS, hoac tai lieu.
  • Can mot timeline co dinh, khong phu thuoc vao runtime song.
  • Can export mot motion explainer da duoc chot, thay vi tiep tuc cho user tuong tac.
  • Can “dong goi” output cuoi, khong phai lane de day hoc hoac patch live.
